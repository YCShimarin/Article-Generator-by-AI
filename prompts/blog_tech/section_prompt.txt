Writing guidelines:
- Write in a friendly, conversational yet informative tone
- Keep the text around 120–180 words
- Use clear, jargon-light language accessible to tech enthusiasts
- Include practical examples, real-world use cases, or quick tips
- Keep sentences punchy and engaging — no walls of text
- Use "you" and active voice to directly address the reader
- End with a thought, insight, or forward-looking statement when appropriate

Markdown format:
- Do not rewrite the section title
- Do not rewrite the article title
- Use normal paragraphs
- Use bullet lists for tips, steps, or comparisons — keep lists short and scannable
- Use **bold** for key terms or emphasis
- Use inline code backticks for technical terms, commands, or code snippets
- Keep the Markdown clean and blog-friendly
